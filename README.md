# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and regional trends. It provides business stakeholders with actionable insights into revenue, profit, product categories, payment methods, customer contributions, and state-wise sales to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data, making it difficult to identify key sales trends and profit drivers. Understanding customer behavior, regional performance, and product profitability is essential for improving revenue and optimizing business strategies.

This dashboard answers critical business questions such as:

- Which states generate the highest sales?
- Which product categories drive the most sales?
- Which sub-categories are the most profitable?
- Who are the top-performing customers?
- Which payment methods are most preferred?
- How does profit vary over time?

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🌍 Regional Sales Performance
- Maharashtra generated the highest sales, followed by Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh.
- Lower-performing states present opportunities for targeted marketing and sales expansion.

### 🛍️ Category Performance
- Clothing accounted for the highest share of product quantity sold.
- Electronics ranked second, while Furniture contributed the lowest sales volume.

### 💰 Profit Analysis
- Profit fluctuated across order dates, indicating seasonal demand and promotional impacts.
- Monitoring profit trends helps identify high-performing sales periods.

### 👥 Customer Analysis
- A small group of customers contributed the highest revenue.
- High-value customers can be targeted with loyalty programs and personalized offers.

### 💳 Payment Analysis
- Cash on Delivery (COD) was the most preferred payment method.
- Digital payments (UPI, Credit Card, and Debit Card) represented a significant portion of transactions.

### 📦 Product Profitability
- Printers generated the highest profit among all sub-categories.
- Some product categories delivered lower profitability, highlighting opportunities for pricing and inventory optimization.
